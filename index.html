ART DIRECTION

PROJECT REFLECTION

What improved the most from Alpha to Final?

The most significant improvement was the **narrative depth and technical complexity**. We moved from a basic 8-panel scroll to a full 16-panel interactive story. The integration of the "AI Mode" transformed the project from a simple comic into a powerful visual metaphor for addiction.

What feedback was most useful?

The feedback regarding **user navigation** was crucial. Adding the dynamic Progress Bar and Panel Counter, based on user testing, drastically improved the UX, as it helped readers stay oriented within the long-scroll format.

What would you still improve?

Given more time, I would implement **advanced CSS parallax effects** to make the transition between panels even more immersive. I would also explore more complex AI training to create a more consistent visual style across the "hallucination" scenes.
