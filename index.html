THE MESSAGE

"Odds Against Life" is not just a story; it's a warning. Gambling addiction doesn't just drain your wallet—it consumes your mind. We created this interactive webtoon to demonstrate the psychological descent into the casino trap. The core message is simple: The house always wins. The only way to survive is to break the cycle.

ART DIRECTION

Why this style? We deliberately chose a dark, gritty aesthetic with harsh neon red accents. This color palette simulates the deceptive, aggressive allure of a casino. The heavy black shadows represent the anxiety and isolation of addiction, stripping away the glamorous illusion to reveal the dangerous reality underneath.
